Nankai University Student Chamber Orchestra

About Us

Founded in 2021, the Nankai University Student Chamber Orchestra (NKCO) was initiated by members of the Nankai Student Symphony Orchestra. Today, the ensemble brings together more than 60 amateur music enthusiasts from non-arts schools across Nankai University. After four years of growth, we remain true to our original aspiration: to share the emotion, warmth, and solace of music with more people.

Our members come from a wide range of schools and perform on strings, winds, and keyboard instruments. Guided by professional mentors, we continue to refine our artistry and strive to present our best in every performance.

NKCO grew out of the Nankai Student Symphony Orchestra (NKO), founded in October 2001. Renowned young conductors Gang Jin and Haochen Wang previously served as resident conductors. Over more than two decades, the orchestra has presented a dedicated concert at the National Centre for the Performing Arts together with the student choir, becoming the first university arts ensemble in China to do so. It has also earned outstanding results in national university arts performance competitions and has regularly presented dedicated concerts on campus.
